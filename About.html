About Off the Shelf

Off the Shelf is a digital repository of first-year nursing students in Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila. It serves as a collection of demonstration videos, lecture lessons and case studies covering subjects in first-year nursing curriculum.

Mission

To provide first-year nursing students easily accessible, comprehensive, and well-structured learning materials that help them establish an excellent foundation for their nursing education by serving as a guide for lesson study, return demonstration preparation, and case study evaluation.

Vision

To be a reliable and innovative center that helps nursing students succeed academically, develop clinical competence, and build confidence as they build careers as knowledgeable and compassionate healthcare professionals.

Meet the Team

We are a group of dedicated nursing students who collaborated to create this Repository Website as part of our Nursing Informatics research project. Our shared goal is to make nursing resources more accessible, well-organized, and user-friendly for both students and professionals. By working together, we combined our skills, knowledge, and creativity to design a platform that supports learning, encourages collaboration, and promotes excellence in nursing education.
